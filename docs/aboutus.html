Our team


ROSWeb was developed during a master degree project, in order to help robotic developers and users to debug and supervise their robotics applications with a easy-to-use interface.

It was developed in LabRoM: Mobile Robots Laboratory, at University of São Paulo, São Carlos, SP - Brazil

Authors: Marco Antonio Nunes da Cunha de Arruda (marcoarruda@usp.br)

Supervisor: Marcelo Becker (becker@sc.usp.br)

Other contributors are listed in the contributors.md file at the GitHub ROSWeb repository
